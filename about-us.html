About Us

At riyaz International, there is a simple philosophy that works – Clients are not our audience, but our success partners. Our success lies in theirs. Hence we’re always in search of opportunities that make the client succeed. We at riyaz International, a leading Human Resource Company take pleasure in assisting our clients to achieve their goals by providing manpower on overseas recruitment basis.

Who We Are

We are an Indian Company backed by 35+ years of result-driven recruitment expertise in manpower recruitment solutions. In addition to manpower recruitment, we also specialize in various Visa processing services. We are motivated by an unfaltering commitment to our client’s requirements.

More About Us

Times are changing and in this race of evolution, we have changed our corporate identity to reflect our new age goals. Yet be enlightened by our age-old ethics. Our services reflect our philosophy that “We Care” for your success. Our commitment to perfection has resulted in high-level satisfaction for both clients and job-seekers.

What’s more, our state-of-the-art well-equipped offices, well equipped and well-trained team, the highly professional selection criteria, all contribute to make us the “Top Most Promising Recruitment Consultant” in the overseas recruitment industry. One of the many reasons why our clients look forward to being associated with us is, that they know we provide them with the right people for the required assignment, whatever the constraints. We look forward to providing our best services to your organization and building a long-lasting positive relationship.
